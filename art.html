Portfolio

From 2021 to 2022 I developed a 3D animation short feature from conception to post-production, passing through animation, texture designing, rendering, rigging, simulation, etc, I did it all by myself. It was important to me to have this kind of experience, to fully understand the pipeline of 3D animation making. The character modeling was done through ZBrush software. Retopology was performed in Maya ensuring the needed edge loops for animation, and textures were painted through Substance Painter, Substance Designer , and Photoshop. The set was modeled directly in Maya, where also all the animation and simulation were performed. Rigging was done with the aid of the DP Auto Rig tool, the rendering was performed through VRay, and post-production was performed in After Effects software.
